la okuntonda n’omukono gwo n’onfuuwamu omwoyo Gwo 
nnasitula omutwe nngenda okulaba ku bikondo bya Arishi 
nga kuwandiikiddwaako “Laa ilaaha illa Laahu Muhammadu 
rasuulu Llaahi” (Teri asinzibwa mu butuufu okuggyako Allah; 
Muhammadi Mubaka wa Allah). Bwe nnalaba ekyo ne mmanya 
nti ku linnya lyo tewagattako linnya okuggyako ery’ekitonde 
ekisinga okubeera ekiganzi gy’oli”. Allah kwe kumugamba 
nti: “Adamu oyogedde mazima, Muhammadi kye kitonde kye 
nsinga okwagala. Onsabye ku lulwe, kati nkusonyiye. Singa si 
Muhammadi sandikusonyiye ”. 3 

Bw’atyo mukama waffe Adamu (Allah amuwe emi- 
rembe) yasobola okusaba ng’ayita mu Mubaka wa Allah, 
Oweekitiibwa Allah n’amusonyiwa. Wuuno Omubaka owee- 
kitiibwa eyali ettondo bwe yajjulukuka ne yeetondeka ku 
mugongo gwa mukama waffe Bulayimu (Allah amuwe emi- 
rembe) Abakaafiiri bwe baamusuula mu muliro okumwo- 
kya Allah yagumufuulira bunnyogovu na mirembe. Ekkula 
ery’ekitiibwa bwe lyayingira ku mugongo gwa Semayiri 
(Ismail) (Allah amuwe emirembe), kitaawe bwe yali agenda 
okumusala Allah yaweereza ekimununula okuva mu ggulu ku 
lwa Muhammadi.

amusaasire amuwe n’emirembe). Mu ngeri eyo mukama waffe 
Musa yayagala abeere mu kibiina kya Muhammadi asobole

Muhammadi (Allah amusaasire amuwe n’emirembe). Kataddaata
