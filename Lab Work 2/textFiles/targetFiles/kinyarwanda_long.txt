Amakuru ageze kuri Shikama mu kanya aturutse i Kigali aravuga ko Senateri Mucyo jadediye(Jean de Dieu) yitabye Imana mu kanya kuri uyu wa mbere tariki ya 3 z' Ukwakira 2016. Mucyo ngo yagize ikibazo ubwo yari ageze muri esikariye mu nyubako y'aho akorera maze yikubita hasi bahita bamujyana kwa muganga, ariko aza gupfira ku bitaro.
Uraho Muvandimwe Dr Joseph,

Iminsi myinshi! Ndakeka ko abasomyi, abakunzi na mwe muri Shikama mwibaza ko naba nkiriho. Yewe ga yewe, ndakomeye pe! uretse ko muri iriya nkubiri yo kwirukana no kwica abasirikare bakomeye ngo Gafuni akunde arambe kabiri, nanjye nahinduriwe imirimo bituma mba ncecetseho gato, ngo hato bataba bari kunyigaho.

Giselansi, ni Segahinda Ngezahabagabo wa Maburakindi na Nyirabaravuga wa hariya mu Gahenerezo wongeye kubasuhuza. Urwandiko rwanjye rw’ubushize ngo rwabagezeho rutinze kuko uwo naruhaye yageze muri USA asanga wavuyeyo werekeje Dubai arwoherejeyo rusanga wagannye muri Singapour barwoherejeyo basanga werekeje  I Hong Kong mu Bushinwa. Ntabwo nakurenganya ni akazi kenshi!! None se ko mu banyarwanda miliyoni 12 zose ntawe wabona ukubera minisitiri w’ububanyi n’amahanga uzi gukora, hari uwakurenganya kuba wigira aho hose?! Ariko se aho kugushimira ubwo bwitange, abenshi ntibirirwa bagusebya ngo uba wagiye guhisha amafaranga yawe!
Kagame Pawulo ari mu bayobozi b'isi bake bigize utumana muri iyi si ya Rurema. Mu mbwirwaruhame ze, nta na rimwe ashobora kurangiza atavuze ko azica abantu, wagirango niwe ufite urufunguzo rw'urupfu n'ubuzima ku banyarwanda! Ntatinya no kuburira abategetsi b'ibihugu duturanye ko nabo azabica nkuko yica abaturage be. Ibi byose akaba abikora yishingikirije Ibinywamaraso bafatanyije guconshomera amaraso y'inzirakarengane kuva muri 1990 n'intwaro zigezweho ahabwa na Leta Zunze Ubumwe z'Amerika(USA), Ubwongereza(UK), Ubudage na Israel.Nyamara ibye bishobora kuzaba nk'ibya Perezida wa Libya warunze ibitwaro agahiryi, akirya icyara kugeza ubwo atangarije kuri Televiziyo y'igihugu cye ko Afrika yiyunze kuva itazemetera umuntu wese uzafata ubutegetsi aciye muri kudeta, we azategeka kugeza apfuye ubutegetsi akabusigira uwo ashaka! Nyagucwa ntabwo yamenye ko umwanzi ava mu nzu nkuko tubivuga mu kinyarwanda. Ibi nibyo bitegereje Kagame uhereye ku kandare gaherutse kuba mu rugo rwe gakongejwe n'Inkoramutima ze zo muri RPF nkuko Shikama ibibagezaho hasi aha.
Iyo  usomye icyegeranyo cya wa mucamanza wo muri Esipanye, Fernando Andreu Melles ku bwicanyi bwakozwe na RPF kuva yatera u Rwanda muri 1990 n'aho ba Mpatsibihugu (USA; UK, Germany, Israel) bayiterekeye mu Rugwiro muri 1994, wibaza niba aba bicanyi koko bari kuvamo abantu bazima babana n'abandi muri sosiyete ya muntu! Ubu bwicanyi burushaho kuba gisoka mu mugongo y'ububwirwa iyo ugeze mu giturage hirya no hino mu Rwanda bakakubwira ibyababayeho ndetse bakaguha n'amazina y'abicanyi ba RPF/RPA/RDF, izina rigaruka cyane rikaba ari iry'umujenerali utazi gusoma no kwandika witwa jenerali Fred Ibingira.
Mu gihe kagame Pawulo yugarijwe n'ibibazo by'urugo rwe hari abantu benshi babyiniye ku rukoma bati : " Awa, sibo babona"! Nyamara uko ibintu bimeze ubu mu Rwanda, buri muntu wese ukunda kiriya gihugu yagombye kujya ku mavi akagisengera kuko isaha iyo ariyo yose Kagame Pawulo ashobora kugicumita inkota nkuko adasiba kubigirira inzirakarengane! Ibi muri Shikama tukaba tubivuga duhereye ku byemezo byuzuye ubuhubutsi bisigaye bifatwa n'umuyobozi nako umutegetsi Pawulo Kagame. Icyemezo cya hafi aheruka gufata kikaba ari ugukuraho ibyapa byose byamamaza byari biri ku mihanda igana ku kibuga cy'indege Mpuzamahanga Gerigori KAYIBANDA Agatsiko kita ubu Kigali International Airport(KIA).Bene ibyapa ubu barira ayo kwarika bakaba barahawe amasaha atanu gusa ngo babe babikuyeho benshi bakaba bari bamaze kushyura imfatakibanza y'umwaka irenga miliyoni icumi y'amafranga y'u Rwanda
Ubu ikiganiro kigezweho mu banyarwanda baba abo hanze y'u Rwanda cyangwa se imbere yarwo, ni injyanishabitsina Muka Pawulo Kagame Nyiramongi Janeti yaba yarakoranye na Gasana Rishari (Richard) wari uhagarariye Pawulo Kagame muri LONI muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe z'Amerika(USA) ndetse hakavukamo n'umwana ubu umaze kuba umusore.  Amaradiyo  y'abanyarwanda yigenga akorera hanze yavuze kuri iyi nkuru biratinda, imbuga nazo si ukwandika zivayo! Muri Shikama twirinze kugira icyo tuvuga kuri iyi nkuru kuko twari tugikora ubushakashatsi tukaba tuzabagezaho IBYAHISHUWE biyerekeyeho mu minsi mike. Ariko se  byaba ari byiza guhangayikira injyanishabitsina yakozwe n'abantu babiri ndetse ikeraho urubuto, cyangwa tugomba guhangayikira umutungo w'igihugu wasahuwe n'umuryango wa Pawulo Kagame Gasana Rishari(Richard) akaba ariwe wari ushinzwe kuwuhisha hirya no hino mu mahanga?
Undi munyamakuru wa Shikama we yafashe ikinyamakuru uko cyakabaye asemura inyandiko yacyo ayikura mu rurimi rw'igiporutugali gikoreshwa muri icyo gihugu ayishyira mu kinywarwanda kugirango adusangize twese.

Ku nyandiko ifite amapaji 7, muramenya amazina y'abantu benshi , baba basirikare, abicanyi b'abasivili KAGAME NA FPR bohereje kwica inzirakarengane muri kariya karere harimo n'abishe Nyakwigendera koloneli Patirike Karegeya. Murasangamo n'amazina y'abanyarwanda bayoboye ubwicanyi buheruka gukorerwa impunzi z'abahutu muri Zambia abu basesekaye i Maputo mu murwa mukuru wa Mozambique. Murasangamo amazina y'abasirikare ba RDF/FPR baheruka kwicirwa muri Mozambique bishwe na Leta mu bitero ihanganyemo n'inyeshyamba za RENAMO, Kagame akaba yarohereje benshi mu ngabo ze RDF/FPR gushyigikira izi nyeshyamba.

Murasangamo amazu yaguzwe na DMI yo guteguriramo ubwicanyi, amatariki y'aho amanama ya DMI yabereye n'amazina y'abari bayarimo kuva muri 2015 kugeza ubu muri 2016.  Ni birebire.

Iyi nyandiko iracyatunganywa, aho ibonekera irabageraho none cyagwa ejo.
