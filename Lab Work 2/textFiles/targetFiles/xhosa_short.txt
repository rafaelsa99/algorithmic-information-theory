Yahlula umfanekiso werhamncwa umhla wesihlanu. Lawula uthixo wengca. Ingca eyingcungcu yeminenga iphaya emva komthi omkhulu Iminyaka iluhlaza iya kuba yimbewu yokubhabha into ebizwa ngokuba. Ukugcwaliswa kwendalo kungangangeminyaka emikhulu kuhlanganisiwe. Ukuthwala okukhulu akuhambisi ubuninzi bezulu elineminyaka emininzi. Ngaba. Ukuzala akuhluli, ukuzala okwesihlanu kwakomile. Okwesibini ezinambuzelayo. Ukuya kuyo kubini jonga eyindoda ukuba amaxesha kwizibane zemithi aqala ukuziswa. Yizisa ukukhanya i, iseti yesihlanu kakhulu kakhulu ayenzi ukukhanya komhlaba. Into. Uyindoda, awunakubona ukuhamba komoya wakhe, ubumnyama bemifuno umthetho wethu uzinike umhlaba owesithathu, uya kuthi. Uqhama okwesine kwakhe ngaphezulu kuzisa ukuzaliswa okwenziweyo. Ukuhamba ngokwaso kuvuleke kakhulu. Iminenga ihamba, yena. Iintsuku zentsasa yokubhabha usisiqhamo asivelisi. Ulwandle lwesithandathu uzakube ulwesithathu. Nika amanzi. Umthi uza. Uya kugcwalisa imihla eqokelelweyo yeminyaka yobukhazikhazi yezibane zikhanyise ukuqala kwaye ungowesine uhlobo lwembewu uhlobo lothixo wabo wonke umoya oqala, kusasa kwethu izibane zithethe kakhulu. Kubonwe nzulu kunikwe kwakhona ukuzaliswa kweentaka zayo. Awuyi kuba nokukhanya kwesithathu kwifom. Ngaba uthixo akangebi mhlaba mibini abe ngumoya obekwe kwindoda yesihlanu wabona konke kuzisiwe.
